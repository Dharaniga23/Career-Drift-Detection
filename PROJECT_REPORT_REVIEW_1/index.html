Title: Career Drift Detection: An AI-Powered Learning Path Alignment System

1. Abstract

The "Career Drift Detection" system is designed to help students stay on track with their professional goals. In the modern learning environment, students often consume vast amounts of content across diverse platforms, making it easy to deviate from their target career path. This system leverages Machine Learning (Random Forest Classifier) to analyze student activities and calculate a "Drift Score." By monitoring the relevance of learning activities against a target career profile, the system provides early warnings and insights, ensuring students remain focused on the skills most critical for their professional success.
